Our Heritage

Our Story

The heart and soul behind Monika Restaurant.

Welcome to Monika Restaurants, where every dish created in our kitchen celebrates authentic West African flavours, culture, and community. Now open in the heart of Deptford, we welcome all ages to an unforgettable, luxurious yet casual dining experience.

We are a seafood and open-grill restaurant, proud to be part of the award-winning 805 Restaurant Group.

We named our restaurant after the famous and iconic Monika fish, a whole grilled popular fish dish to honour an incredible dish that was created by the pioneers of 805 restaurant Mr Frederick James and Mrs Damaris James.

Monika Fish has now become symbolic and a traditional favourite in the Nigerian community. Monika fish represents the rich culinary heritage and high standards that the 805 Restaurant Group is known for.

This whole grilled croaker fish is marinated in our special home seasonings and spices, then chargrilled to lock in a delicious smoky flavour that complements the dish. The fish is finished with a generous amount of our spicy Monika sauce.
